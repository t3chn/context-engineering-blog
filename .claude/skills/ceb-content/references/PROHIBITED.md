# Prohibited Patterns

- Excessive emoji (max 1-2 per post)
- Author signature at end
- CTA ("subscribe!", "read more!")
- Motivational tone ("You can do it!")
- Promotional language ("The best way!")
- Condescending ("Just do...")
- Clickbait headlines
- Empty filler paragraphs
- Bullet lists in Telegram (visual noise)
- Bold/italic formatting in Telegram
